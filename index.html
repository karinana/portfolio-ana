About Me

Artist turned Software Engineer

In college I followed my passion and studied Visual Arts with a concentration on design and photography. I used my design oriented thinking after college in jobs such as desktop publishing, and editing and production in a photo lab. I became interested in web design and taught myself HTML and CSS to create personal websites for myself and artist friends to showcase our portfolios. Creating websites grew to be another passion when I realized that I loved learning how things worked online. After the pandemic began and I started spending more time at home, I dived back in and started adding to my coding skills and bringing myself up to date on new technologies. Most recently, I have worked in teams with other programmers, created websites for clients, and worked on my own full stack web apps. I love the lifelong learning that comes along with software engineering.
